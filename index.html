About Us

American Stone is founded on Christian principles. We are committed to provide our customers with a pleasurable business experience based on honesty, integrity, and transparency. We have the largest inventory of Granite, Marble, Quartz, Soapstone and Limestone in Cape Cod, and with our many years of experience in the industry, you can feel confident that your project is in good hands. Satisfied clients and referrals are the core of our business and we strive to meet your every expectation.
